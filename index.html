We run regular weekly classes, social dances, and participate in national and international WCS events. Whether you want to learn, improve, or just dance for fun, you're in the right place.

The dance

What is West Coast Swing?

West Coast Swing is a partner dance rooted in American swing tradition. It's improvisational, musical, and endlessly adaptable — danced to everything from blues and pop to hip-hop and country. No two dances are ever the same.

🎵

Music Freedom

WCS works with almost any contemporary music — from slow blues to uptempo pop. The dance follows the song, not the other way around.

🤝

Partner Connection

Built on lead-follow communication, WCS teaches you to listen, respond, and create a conversation through movement.

🌱

Beginner Friendly

You don't need a partner or prior experience. Our beginner series takes you from zero to the social floor in just a few sessions.

✨

Improvisation

Once you know the basics, WCS becomes a creative conversation — every dance a unique, in-the-moment expression.

🌍

Global Community

WCS is danced worldwide. Your skills travel with you — events, workshops, and socials happen on every continent.

❄️

Arctic Edition

We dance under the northern lights, the midnight sun, and everything in between. Tromsø adds its own magic to every social.
